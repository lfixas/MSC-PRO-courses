Cup

A cup is a small container typically used for drinking water or other beverages. It can be made of various materials, such as glass, ceramic, or plastic.

Bottle

A bottle is a container designed to hold liquids, including water. It comes in different sizes and shapes and is commonly used for storing and transporting water.

Teapot

A teapot is a vessel with a spout, lid, and handle, used for brewing and serving tea. It is essential for making hot water for tea preparation.

Swimming Pool

A swimming pool is a man-made structure filled with water, primarily used for recreational swimming and other water-related activities.

Waterfall

A waterfall is a natural phenomenon where water flows rapidly over a vertical drop in the terrain, creating a stunning display of cascading water.

Ocean

The ocean, often referred to as the "oceanic" or "marine" environment, is a vast and interconnected body of saltwater that covers more than 70% of the Earth's surface.
